Span of two vectors

The span of u and v is every point you can reach as a combination a·u + b·v. Two independent arrows reach the whole plane; two parallel ones reach only a line. See span & linear independence.

vector u vector v

Key idea: span is everything two arrows can build by stretching and adding. The dimension is how many independent directions you genuinely have.
